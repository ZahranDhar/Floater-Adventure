Our Team

MI

Mohammad Ishaq Shalla

Founder

Ishq Shalla, the founder and guiding force behind Floater Tours, hails from the heart of Downtown Srinagar, Kashmir — a land known for its timeless beauty, rich culture, and deep-rooted hospitality. With over 30 years of experience in the travel and tourism industry, Ishq Shalla has turned a lifelong passion for exploration into a purpose-driven venture that brings people closer to the world — and to themselves.

Having witnessed both the charm and challenges of travel across continents, he founded Floater Tours with a clear mission: to curate authentic, immersive, and safe travel experiences that go beyond sightseeing. His decades of hands-on expertise, local wisdom, and global insight have shaped Floater Tours into a trusted name in escorted group travel, known for its warmth, attention to detail, and deeply personal touch.

Under his leadership, Floater Tours lives by the core belief:

Escape. Explore. Experience.

A journey with us isn’t just a trip — it’s a transformation.

HR

Harris Riyaz

Co-Founder

Harris Riyaz, the co-founder of Floater Adventure, is a dynamic travel enthusiast based in the serene surroundings of Hazratbal, Srinagar, Kashmir. With over 7 years of hands-on experience in the adventure and tourism industry, Harris brings youthful energy, modern insight, and a deep-rooted love for the outdoors to the heart of the company. Growing up in the culturally rich and naturally stunning landscapes of Kashmir, he developed a strong connection with nature and a passion for helping others experience it in its purest form. His vision for Floater Adventure is to offer thrill-seekers, explorers, and nature lovers unforgettable journeys that are as safe as they are soul-stirring. Known for his detail-oriented planning and people-first approach, Harris continues to shape Floater Adventure into a brand that blends authenticity, adrenaline, and unforgettable memories.

AR

Abhay Ray

Partner, CEO

Abhay Ray, the Partner and CEO of Floater Tour and Travel, is a passionate leader based in Ghaziabad, Delhi, with over 5 years of experience in the travel and tourism industry. With a sharp business acumen and a deep understanding of traveler expectations, Abhay plays a pivotal role in steering the company’s vision and operations. His strategic leadership, innovative mindset, and commitment to excellence have helped Floater Tour and Travel grow into a trusted name in curated group travel. Abhay is deeply driven by the belief that travel should be both enriching and accessible, and he works tirelessly to ensure every journey with Floater is seamless, personalized, and unforgettable. Under his guidance, the company continues to expand its reach while staying true to its core values: Escape. Explore. Experience. 

I

Irfan

Marketing Head

Irfan, the Marketing Head of Floater Tour and Travel, brings over 6 years of industry experience and a deep understanding of travel trends and digital engagement. Based in Rohini, Delhi, Irfan is the creative force behind the company’s brand visibility, outreach campaigns, and customer engagement strategies. With a strong background in both traditional and digital marketing, he plays a key role in connecting Floater’s unique travel experiences with the right audiences across India and beyond. Irfan’s passion for storytelling and innovation ensures that every campaign not only informs but inspires. His work has been instrumental in building Floater’s reputation as a reliable, experience-driven, and customer-focused travel brand.
